Our Story

민화 뷰티
Minhwa Beauty

Minhwa (민화) — the folk paintings of Korea — captured the beauty of everyday life with warmth, color, and care. We carry that same spirit into skincare: approachable luxury, rooted in heritage, crafted for the modern ritual.

Born at the intersection of Korean hanbang tradition and African botanical wisdom, Minhwa Beauty creates formulas that honor both cultures — bridging continents through the universal language of skin.

"Where the patience of Korean beauty meets the richness of African nature."
